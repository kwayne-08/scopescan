Property scope scanning

Turn your property photos into SCOPE analysis scans using AI technology for object detection. Scope your properties in minutes - not hours!

The property management challenge

Managing property scopes is an essential but often tedious task for property managers. It can take up much of their time, leaving little room for other essential tasks. However, what if there was a way to automate property scope reports using vision AI that scans the property and identifies its elements? This would free up a significant amount of time for managers to focus on other important tasks.

All it takes is a 30-minute photo shoot of the property and another 30 minutes to scan, review, and finalize the scope report. With this time saved, property managers can now begin to think about how to better use their time and resources to achieve their goals.
