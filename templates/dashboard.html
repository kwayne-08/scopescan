Scan-A-Scope

Upload your property photos and run our specially trained object detection model for household objects. You will receive a SCOPE report on your property saving you hours of paperwork.  Download your report in either a CSV or PDF file

Follow the steps below, download your report, and take the rest of the day off!

First Step:  For accurate data collection for the SCOPE report, please scan photos by room. Begin by uploading photos for each room separately and indicate which room they belong to. Remember to provide the project address below and select the appropriate room for each set of images.

Once you've uploaded photos for a room, run a Scope Scan before moving on to the next room. To avoid mixing up pictures from different rooms, click the "Clear Images" button before processing a new set of photos.
